NEW Bat Rules for 2015

  • The QSBL will now be allowing the use of bats which have the USSSA stamp.
  • All Bats marked with the "NEW (1.20 BPF) USSSA STAMP ON THE TAPER OF THE BAT" will be allowed in the QSBL.
  • ALL new bats manufactured after January 1, 2012 "MUST CARRY THE NEW USSSA STAMP".
  • Click here to view a "Cheat Sheet" displaying which Stamps are legal and which aren't.
  • Older bats must still conform to the old ASA Bat Rules : ASA Non-Approved Bat list - Updated 4/17/2014 - Click here to View ASA Non-Approved Bat List
